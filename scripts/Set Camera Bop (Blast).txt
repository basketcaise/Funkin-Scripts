Bops the camera lololol!!!

Value 1: Camera Bop Multiplier
Set either cameras (camGame or camHUD) bop
multiplier by using a comma to separate the two values
eg. '0.015, 0.03'

Value 2: Camera Bop Type
1 makes it bop every beat
2 makes it bop every OTHER beat
3 makes it bop AND turn every beat
Leave blank to disable the event
